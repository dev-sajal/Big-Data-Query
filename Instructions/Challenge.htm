Welcome to big data engineering hands on challenge. At high level below are your tasks for this challenge

1)      Ingest data into spark environment (Extract)

2)      Clean and transform the data (Transform)

3)      Analyze data and create custom reports (Analysis)

4)      Write the results to storage (Load)
